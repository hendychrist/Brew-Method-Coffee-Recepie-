Step 01 · Prep

Set the stage.

Position the CT62, add your filter paper, and heat the Stagg EKG to temperature. Scroll to begin.

Step 02 · Bloom

Open the coffee.

Pour 45ml at 97°C. Stir 10 times. Seal the dripper. CO₂ gases escape — flavour unlocks.

97°C 0:00 → 0:55 Stir 10×
Step 03 · Steep

Build the body.

Unseal. Pour 92°C to 105ml cumulative. Stir 5 times. Re-seal at 1:10 for the second steep.

92°C 0:55 → 2:10 Stir 5×
Step 04 · Finish

Cool the close.

Pour 70°C water to 225ml total. Low temperature preserves clarity and removes harshness.
